How to turn $100 into $1,000,000 summary

Introduction: Why $1,000,000 dollars?

The value of a million dollars can vary based on who is talking about it. A million dollars to a multimillionaire, or especially a billionare, is basically chump change. To us, however, 1 million dollars is still a lot of money. Saving up that much money will require commitment, hard work, and a little sacrifice. "Anyone can do it, But most people don't". Most people say no with their actions even if this much money could help give you:

  • Freedom: More money = more choices. Period.
  • A Safety Net: Having money & an emergency fund is a protection against the many unpredictable misfortunes and hardships of life.
  • A chance to Help others: You, and everybody around you can benefit from you having money. You can also donate your money to arts, charities, schools, etc.

Dream big and start saving up to that $1 million and make sure to have fun while doing it.
